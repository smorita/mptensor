If index_begin[r]==index_end[r], this rank is not sliced. T.set_slice( slice(T,i,j), i,j) does nothing. For example, T.set_slice(A,Index(1,0,3),Index(4,0,6)); is equal to T[1:4,:,3:6]=A[:,:,:] in Python.

template<template< typename > class Matrix, typename C>
Axes mptensor::Tensor< Matrix, C >::axes_map
private

Map of axes for lazy evaluation of transpose.

This is the inverse permutation of axes given in transpose(), i.e. axes_map[axes[i]]=i. The i-th index of the orignal tensor is moved to the (axes_map[i])-th index of the transposed tensor.
